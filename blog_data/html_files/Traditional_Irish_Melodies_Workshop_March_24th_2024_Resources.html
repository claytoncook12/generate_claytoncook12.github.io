Jannell C. and John W. conducted a Traditional Irish Melodies workshop at the Louisville Folk School. Talked about the banjo in irish music, fathers of the irish banjo and taught some irish tunes. Below are some audio and sheet music references.

Polka: Bhios La I bPort Lairge

Jigs: The Cordal and Vincent Campbell's

Polka: Siege of Ennis

Reels: Jug of Punch - Watchmaker - Shaskeen

Madame Maxwell (aka Planxty Miss Maxwell, by Turlough O'Carolan)
