Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

+

No prior experience is necessary. We will be learning tunes slowly by ear. All are welcome to join the class.

Do I need to purchase a instrument before the class?

+

Yes, you will need to have an instrument before the class begins.

What melody instruments can I play in the class?

+

The primary instruments that will be used to teach during the class will be tin whistle, flute, and fiddle. These are the instruments that the instructors play and will be teaching on. If you play one another melody instrument, such as mandolin, guitar, etc., you can attend the class and learn the tunes with us. We may be limited with instrument specific knowledge, but I will do my best to help you learn the tunes.

Where can I purchase a tin whistle?

+

Here are a few links for quality whistles available to purchase online:

  • The Trad by Tony Dixon - Key of D - Great Budget Option
  • Generation Whistle - Key of D- Great Beginner Whistle
  • Generation Brass Whistle - Key of D
  • Waltons Brass Mellow Whistle - Key of D

What type of music will be taught in the class?

+

We will be learning Irish traditional tunes such as polkas, jigs, reels, and slip jigs.

Will there be sheet music?

+

Sheet music will be provided after the lesson, but the tunes will be taught by ear.
